Classic survival experience

Keep it simple, keep it fun

For modpacks as classic as the Tekkit packs, adding heaps of modifications to gameplay via plug-ins doesn't appeal to us. We keep the experience as pure of a multiplayer survival server as possible.

Community focused

The players make the server

Hosting a server alone isn't enough. We aim to keep an entire community dedicated to Tekkit alive for years to come.
